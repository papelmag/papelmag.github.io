Queremos Rock!!! @ The Vatican

Martes 18 de Mayo de 2010 23:52 Papel Mag

 

     The Vatican brings you a Dyonisian night of entertainment filled with music and plentiful drinking spirits with Queremos Rock!!! on May 20th. The evening includes an open bar and from the stage, the vibes of DJ Red, Cuasp, and Silvano.

     Doors open at 10:00 PM, kicking off the open bar until 1:00 AM for a $12 cover charge. The Vatican's friendly staff encourages you to dress the part. Ample parking available.

 

Venue: The Vatican (703 Calle Jordán, Santurce, Puerto Rico)

Date: Thursday, May 20th, 2010.

Time: Doors open at 10:00 PM.

Cover charge: $12.00 (Open bar from 10:00 PM to 1:00 AM)

Contact: (787) 360-1715
